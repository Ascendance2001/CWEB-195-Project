Photography Request Form

Welcome to the contact form for requesting the use of Gerald Saul's photography work. Gerald Saul, an esteemed experimental photographer and faculty member at the University, has an extensive portfolio of captivating and thought-provoking images.

If you are interested in utilizing Gerald Saul's photography for a project, exhibition, publication, or any other purpose, please fill out the form below. Provide your name, email address, and a brief description of the reason for requesting Gerald Saul's work. We appreciate your interest and will review your request promptly.

Gerald Saul's unique perspective and artistic vision make his photography an excellent choice for various creative endeavors. We kindly ask that you provide a clear explanation of how you plan to use his work and any relevant details about your project.
